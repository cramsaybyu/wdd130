Jamaica

Jamaica is an island located in the Caribbean Sea. Its the third largest island of the Greater Antilles. Its capital is Kingston to the South, a very busy commercial area. The interior is mountainous and get cold at time for a tropical country. The official language is English but Patois is commonly spoken. A broken English and combination of East African words.
