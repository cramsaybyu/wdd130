About Us | Cool River Rafting Co.

Cool River Rafting Company, we strive to give our customers the best and thrilling rafting experience. Our team are experienced and certified instructors with top safety records.

History

Established in 1974 with one location in Manchester, Central Jamaica. Operated by Basil Ramsay and his wife, Cynthia Vernon-Ramsay and one staff from the local area. After about ten years they opened a second shop in St. Ann Bay. The shop was located near the the Dunn's River Falls staffed by about four locals form the community. It is currently operated by Basil Ramsay's son and daughter after there retirement in 2018.
